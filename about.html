Our History

AcuraCare Sports Orthopedics

Founded in 2014 by physical therapists and orthopedic surgeons, AcuraCare was built to serve active individuals who need specialized, sports-specific medical care. We believed that standard hospital systems lacked the dynamic, exercise-based tracking required to get athletes safely back to their original performance standards.

Today, our clinic features a state-of-the-art diagnostic biomechanics lab, full-scale rehabilitation gyms, and on-site minor surgery consultation suits, hosting over 25 healthcare professionals.

Our Mission

To provide elite, comprehensive joint care and active physical therapy to all levels of athletes, enabling rapid and safe return to active lifestyles through evidence-based medicine, personalized coaching, and advanced technologies.

Our Vision

To be the premier clinical recovery partner for sports injury restoration globally, leading the industry in dynamic joint tracking, minimally invasive arthroscopy, and integrated medical-physical therapy collaboration.
