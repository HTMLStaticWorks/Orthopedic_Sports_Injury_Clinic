Transit & Directions

By Subway: Take the Green Line (B Branch) directly to Boston University Central Station. The clinic entrance is located 2 blocks east, inside the Medical Rehabilitation Complex.

By Bus: Route 57 stops directly in front of the center complex. Look for the AcuraCare Sports blue sign beside the main revolving doors.

Parking & Accessibility

Valet Services: Dedicated medical valet is accessible at the main driveway drop-off between 8:00 AM and 5:00 PM for all orthopedic patients.

Self-Parking Lot: Free 2-hour validated visitor parking is located in Lot C directly behind the clinic. Bring your gate slip to the front desk coordinate validation.

Booking FAQ

What is the appointment cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours notice for any cancellations or schedule changes. Late cancellations or missed appointments may incur a $50 administrative fee.

What documents should I bring to my initial consultation?

Please bring a valid photo ID, your active health insurance card, a copy of any recent diagnostic scans (MRI, CT, X-Ray) on CD, and a list of your current prescriptions.

Do you accept my insurance provider?

We partner with most major medical and athletic insurance providers. Please view our accepted providers list on the home page or contact our pre-authorization coordinators to confirm your coverage details.

How can I access my customized rehabilitation plans?

Once prescribed, you can download and print all your exercise plans, timelines, and guides directly from the patient portal dashboard. Log in using your email and password.
