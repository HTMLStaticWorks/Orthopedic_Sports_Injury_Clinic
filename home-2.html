Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our athletic physiotherapy and recovery programs.

Do I need a physician's referral to start physical therapy?

In most cases, no. Under direct access laws, you can begin physical therapy without a doctor's script. However, some insurance plans require a referral for billing coverage. Contact our billing specialists to verify your plan constraints.

How long does each physiotherapy session last?

A standard session is approximately 45 to 60 minutes. Your initial assessment, where we construct your joint strength baseline, will require about 75 minutes.

How many times a week will I need rehabilitation?

Depending on your injury type and severity, appointments range from 1 to 3 sessions per week. We provide digital home training guides to make sure recovery continues between clinic appointments.

What should I wear to my rehabilitation appointments?

Wear loose-fitting athletic clothing (shorts, t-shirts, sports shoes) that allows easy access to your treated joint (knee, shoulder, elbow) for motion tracking and manual therapy.
