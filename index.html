Our Orthopedic Recovery Timeline

A structured, medically supervised journey designed to guide you safely from initial trauma back to peak physical function.

Phase 1

Comprehensive Evaluation

We begin with diagnostic testing, dynamic range-of-motion assessments, and advanced imaging (MRI/X-Ray) to locate exact joint and tissue damages.

Phase 2

Custom Surgical / Treatment Plan

For surgical cases, we map out minimally invasive arthroscopy. Non-surgical options include targeted orthobiologics or custom braces.

Phase 3

Active Physiotherapy

Once healed post-op, you enter physical therapy. Focus lies on range-of-motion restoring, muscle activation, and pain management.

Phase 4

Strength & Agility Retraining

Gradual progression into sport-specific drills, power training, biomechanical alignment adjustments, and active core stabilizing.

Phase 5

Return to Competition

Final functional biomechanics clearance test. We certify you fit for dynamic competition and supply customized injury prevention guidelines.
